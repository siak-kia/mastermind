Mastermind Game

How to play mastermind: The computer selects the pattern of 4 colors( for example 1 3 4 5). you have to guess the pattern with the hints you are given in each move. In the first column of boxes you have to enter numbers from 0 to 7 separated by space and then press Check button, the number of black rectangles shows the number of digits with correct color and AND correct position and white rectangles shows the number of digits which are correct but in wrong position for example if you enter 1 2 3 4 and target is 1 2 4 7 two black rectangles are shown for 1 and 2 and 1 white rectangle is shown for 4.
